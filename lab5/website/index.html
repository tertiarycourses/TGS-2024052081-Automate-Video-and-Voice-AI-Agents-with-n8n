Frequently asked questions

Ava answers from exactly these six topics — and says so when a question falls outside them.

How long does delivery take, and what does it cost?

Standard delivery is 3 to 5 working days. Express delivery is next working day if you order before 2 PM. Delivery is free on orders above $50; below that it is a flat $5. We deliver across Singapore only.

What is your returns policy?

You may return any item within 30 days of delivery, unused and in its original packaging. Refunds go back to the original payment method within 5 to 7 working days. Opened personal-care items cannot be returned.

Do products come with a warranty?

All electrical appliances carry a 1-year local manufacturer warranty; Dyson and KitchenAid carry 2 years. Keep your order number — it is your proof of purchase.

Which payment methods do you accept?

PayNow, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay and Google Pay. We do not accept cash on delivery. Instalments are available on orders above $300.

Do you install large appliances?

Yes. Washing machines and air conditioners include free basic installation. Installation is scheduled within 3 working days of delivery, and the old unit can be hauled away for $20.

Can I get a price match?

Yes. If you find the identical item cheaper at a local retailer within 7 days of your purchase, we refund the difference. Send the order number and a link to the competitor's listing.

How the voice agent works

1Browser

The page starts a WebRTC call with the Vapi Web SDK using your public key.

2Vapi

Vapi runs the assistant: speech-to-text, the model, and the voice back to you.

3Grounding

The assistant answers only from the FAQ in its prompt, and refuses anything outside it.

A public key is designed to be visible in the browser and can only start calls. Your private key manages assistants and must never appear in front-end code.
